Paris, France

The City of Lights. Paris is a classic choice for a reason. It's home to iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum, as well as a vibrant culture, delicious food, and beautiful architecture.

London, England

Steeped in history and culture, London has something for everyone. From Buckingham Palace and the Houses of Parliament to world-class museums and theaters, there's no shortage of things to see and do in this dynamic city.

This beautiful island is a paradise for beach lovers, nature enthusiasts, and spiritual seekers. With its stunning beaches, lush rain forests, and ancient temples, Bali is a place to relax, explore, and connect with your inner self.
